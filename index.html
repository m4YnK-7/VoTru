VoTru

Blockchain Based Voting System

Elections are crucial events in a true democracy. But today, even the most democratic countries have problems with their electoral processes- hacking and rigging of electronic voting machines, electoral fraud, and violence at the polling stations. Here, we are trying to give the best possible solution: a decentralized polling system. VoTru serves as a comprehensive platform for both political representatives and voters alike. Individuals can register as representatives of political parties, enabling them to participate actively in the democratic process.
